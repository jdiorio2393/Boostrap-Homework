A Website Done By A... Cat??

My name is Jake, and I am a cat. And before you ask, YES cats CAN become web designers. While you humans think we are "always plotting to kill you," we really are... meh well we are, but I digress.

My dream is to one day be the first cat in space. I know it seems far fetched but hey, you're looking at a website designed by a cat so I think we are a little past that.

Web design is a great way to make money, all while staying home, eating my kibble and playing with lush catnip toys. Yea my life is pretty great. But this life of leasure will one day end when I am the first CAT IN SPACE!

Thank you very much for listening to my story dear human.
